Commodities Trader, Analyst & Speaker

Nik has over 14 years financial markets experience held in various positions from trading to investment research and advisory.

As internationally respected commodities trading expert, Nik’s opinions on the markets are routinely sought by major financial television channels and publications. Nik is also regularly invited to speak at numerous trading conferences sharing his insights on the commodities markets with financial institutions, investment banks and traders interested in the metals, energies and agricultural sectors.
